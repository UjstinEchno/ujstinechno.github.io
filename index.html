Justin
Enoch

I'm a Romanian-born, LA-based sound designer, composer, and interdisciplinary artist.

I work with the Post Collective Post Mambo, produce music as Romanian Soundbath and run Radio Benska on KCHUNG Public at the Geffen Contemporary

Currently, I'm pursuing an MFA in immersive mixing under David Novack at Universidade Lusofona in Portugal.
